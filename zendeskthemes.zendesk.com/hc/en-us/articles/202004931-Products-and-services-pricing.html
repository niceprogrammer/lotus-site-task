Products and services pricing

Michael Reid
December 06, 2014 07:28
Follow

Where does the price come from, what is included and what’s not, please find out from this paragraph.

  Fully transparent prices

Themes pricing

You can find the price for each of our themes in the catalog. The price of a theme includes its purchase and installation to your Zendesk Help Center. Any Zendesk template or service will cost you exactly the price specified in the catalog.

Services pricing

  1. Some of the services (such as branding) are rather standard, you can find the price for each of them in our catalog.
  2. The cost of other services (such as customization) depends on the level of difficulty and is calculated separately in each case. Just share your customization requirements with us, and we will calculate the cost.
  3. During the checkout of your order, you might see some additional offers appear, such as services or option, you can add to your cart, but we do not have any hidden cost involved in our template sales.
  4. If you decide to order additional options later, it will come as a separate order.

Free services

Some of the services (such as installation) are free, due to they don't require a lot of time  .

  No hidden payment

  1. No monthly fee! All prices are one-off.
  2. No extra payments.
  3. No taxes.
  4. No commissions.
  5. It doesn’t matter whether you use an existing Help Center or create a new one, how much content or how many agents you have.
  6. The prices you see in the catalog or cart are ultimate.

  Note

Zendesk plan price is not included in the template price. You’ll need to purchase the Zendesk plan subscription and template separately.
