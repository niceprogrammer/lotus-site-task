Themes compatibility

Michael Reid
December 06, 2014 07:10
Follow

This article shows how all-purpose our themes are.

  Our themes are compatible with:

  • Various devices:
    • Fullscreen desktops,
    • Portable devices;
  • All popular web-browsers:
    • Google Chrome,
    • Firefox,
    • Safari,
    • Opera,
    • IE11+;
  • Zendesk plans:
    • Team,
    • Professional,
    • Enterprise,
    • Elite;
  • Applicable Zendesk versions:
    • Help Center;
  • Languages:
    • All the 40+ languages supported by Zendesk;
  • Help Center pages:
    • All the Help Center pages.

  Important

  • Zendesk Essential plan doesn't support customized templates, so our themes are not compatible with it.
  • The Web Portal (the previous version of Help Center) is not compatible with our Themes! Learn more about different versions of Zendesk here.
