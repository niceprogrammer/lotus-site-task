Branding service FAQ

Michael Reid
December 21, 2014 14:35
Follow

Learn what can be done with this type of service.

  Questions

Branding service is the fastest and cost-saving way to make your Help Center look like a part of your main website. Explore some details below.

What is included?

The offer includes the standard list of basic changes: we can change the colors of Help Center elements, use your corporate font and logo, change hero image and custom icons.

You just need to provide us with the colors, fonts, and images that you would like to use. We can use your company brand book if you have one, or we can take colors and images from your main website.

If you need profound changes, we can offer the Customization service.

Open product page
What is not included?

Layout changes and any other customizations out of a standard branding list are not included.

If you need profound changes, we can offer the Customization service.

How much will it cost?

Take a look at our Product page to learn the pricing information.

How long will it take?

The entire Branding process takes around 1–5 working days. We will create a demo Help Center where you will be able to check all the changes. Then we can install the modified theme to your Help Center (free of charge).

How are the changes deployed?

We create a demo Help Center where you can check all the changes. Afterward, we install the chosen theme on your Help Center where you can test everything with your real content in a preview mode. You check the changes and publish everything when you are ready, the changes are published immediately.

Can it be purchased later?

No problem to buy it later as a separate order. You can do it whenever you want.
