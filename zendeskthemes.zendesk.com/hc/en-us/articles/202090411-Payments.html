Payments

Michael Reid
December 21, 2014 15:56
Follow

We made the payment process convenient to you, please see the instructions how to do it fast and easy.

All global payment methods

We accept all global debit, credit cards and PayPal payments:

  • Debit and credit cards            
  • PayPal Personal and Premier  
  • PayPal Corporate  

Reliable platforms

We use world leaders payment platforms only. Your payments are processed securely by   PayPal.com or   2checkout.com gateways.

Payment instructions

Cards PayPal Personal or Premier PayPal Corporate
Use "Credit card" option in the Cart. Clicking the "Complete order" button will redirect you to 2checkout page. You need to input all the details at the "Credit/Debit Card" tab.

Use "PayPal" option in the Cart. Clicking the "Complete order" button will open PayPal window where you need to input all the details.
Use "Credit card" option in the Cart. Clicking the "Complete order" button will redirect you to 2checkout page. You need to input all the details at the "PayPal" tab.

If you are experiencing problems with payment gateways, please inform us. It would be nice if you could send us a copy of bug description or a screenshot.
