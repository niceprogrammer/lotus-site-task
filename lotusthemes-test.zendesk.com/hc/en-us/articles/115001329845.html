Is your support free or should I pay for your assistance?

Andrey Metelsky
February 16, 2017 10:39
Follow

If your request is related to the template not functioning correctly, an error in the download or general getting started instructions, feel free to ask for our support. We limit the questions to issues related to getting the template installed and working. Anything else is beyond the scope of a template sale.
